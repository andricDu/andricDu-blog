## Summary

A Javascript/D3 based visualization for visualizing genetic alteration occurences affecting sets of donors.

This library was built as part of [OICR](http://oicr.on.ca/)'s efforts under the umbrella of Overture and OncoJS and saw use in platforms such as the ICGC Data Portal.

Licensed as Apache-2.0

Source: https://github.com/oncojs/oncogrid
